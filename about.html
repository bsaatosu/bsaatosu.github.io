History

This organization was formed in 2016 with the aim to unite all students and alumni of University of Virginia, and friends and families around the surrounding areas who belong to (either by citizenship or family heritage) or are interested in Bangladesh and share closeness to the cause of the birth of the great nation, Bangladesh and her prosperity. Utilizing this platofrm we would like to represent Bangladesh (History, Culture, Literature, Social values, Scholarly achievements, Concerns & Prospects) in all aspects in a formal way (as permitted by regulations for student organizations) at University of Virginia.
